3. Click & Vote improvements - Electronic voting with Blockchain technology

Administration, updating and deployment of improvements to the Click & Vote Platform, which seeks to facilitate the remote voting process in shareholders' meetings, through a secure and private tool under Blockchain technology, which guarantees greater security, trust and traceability of information.

The platform uses Blockchain technology, which complies with fundamental principles to guarantee that the exercise of voting respects the rights of voters, regulatory requirements and gives the issuer the confidence that the entire process will be carried out in an integral and secure manner.

4. Cross Listing Market with BVC

I led the implementation of the cross-listing modality ("Cross Listing") that allows investors, both institutional and individual, to buy and sell Colombian securities (in CLP and/or USD) listed on the Colombian Stock Exchange (BVC) through a national brokerage firm and under local rules directly on the Santiago Stock Exchange (BCS). This initiative seeks to increase the liquidity and competitiveness of the local market, and is part of an agreement signed between the BCS and the BVC for the dual stock listing in both stock exchanges.
